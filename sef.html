Sef

One of Dutch rap's chief voices

Saturday 13

You can always count on Dutch-Moroccan artist Sef to capture the moment urgently and sincerely.

With his deeply impressive performances on big festival stages last summer, and a sold-out club tour still fresh in our minds, Sef has solidified himself as one of Dutch rap’s leading lights. From his beloved hip-hop staple De Leven to latest LP lieve monsters – a dark but danceable tale of collective despair – Yousef Gnaoui enters Best Kept Secret with a surplus of stories, stirring energy and moments that’ll stick with you.
